The Complex Network Lab started its work as a Computer Network Research Lab in August 1996 under supervision of Dr. AnaLoui. The lab got involved in projects such as Grid Computing, Internet Congestion Control, P2P network traffic engineering and then enterd to the topics close to the complex networks.


The complex network research lab is focused currently in the research area including Computer Networks, Social Networks, Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, and Virtualization.
